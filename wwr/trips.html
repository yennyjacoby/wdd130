Detailed Trips

Rapid Rush at Blue River

Perfect for thrill-seekers, Rapid Rush at Blue River is our fastest and most exciting white-water adventure. This 3-hour trip takes you through Class III–IV rapids, sharp turns, and heart-pounding drops. Designed for 4–8 people per raft, guests must be at least 12 years old and comfortable in moving water. Expect to get soaked, laugh a lot, and leave with serious bragging rights.

Family Flow

A relaxing and scenic float ideal for beginners and families with kids. Family Flow lasts 2 hours and glides through gentle Class I–II waters with plenty of calm stretches to enjoy wildlife, photos, and sunshine. Each raft holds 4–6 people, and children as young as 5 years old are welcome. This is the perfect introduction to rafting for first-timers.

Stormwater

Our most extreme experience! Stormwater Trip Adventure is a 4½-hour expedition into powerful Class IV rapids with big waves and technical maneuvering. Each raft carries 6-8 adventurous paddlers plus a professional guide. Recommended for ages 15+ and for guests in good physical condition. If you want adrenaline, this is the one.

Splash Point

Short on time but still want excitement? Splash Point is a 1½-hour quick adventure featuring playful Class II–III rapids and splash zones that cool you off instantly. Rafts accommodate 5–7 people, making it great for friends and groups. No experience needed — just bring energy and a willingness to get wet.

Giggle Rapids

The most fun per mile! Giggle Rapids is a 2½-hour laughter-filled trip full of bouncy Class II waves and friendly competitions between rafts. Each raft holds 4–8 people and is suitable for ages 8+. Expect water fights, guide games, and lots of smiles — it’s our most popular group outing.
